    We are the world’s most fashionable search engine. Our sole purpose is for you to find exactly what you’re looking for across only the very best retailers and brands, from department to boutique. By seamlessly merging content and commerce, we are here to change the way you shop.

    Our Dna

    We are dynamic and contemporary in our mindset. We love fashion and style and like to celebrate all of life’s defining moments. We are informative and relevant and operate within the categories of women’s, men’s, kids’, and home. We have an extensive influencer network but love the fact that we are still an insider secret. We are smart, confident, and happy with an easy, can-do California attitude.

    Our 9-to-5

    ShopStyle is based in San Francisco and has localized sites in the US, the UK, France, Germany, Japan, Canada, and Australia, but our teams and customers are all around the world. We have a bold Silicon Valley outlook on developing transformative technology for fashion and strive to do this bigger and better every day.
